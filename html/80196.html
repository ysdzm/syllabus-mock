授業の目的・達成目標

Biopolymers, such as proteins and polynucleotides, will be targeted as functional polymers in this class,

Particularly, photochemical aspects of biopolymers will be focused on.

Students will study the basis of photochemistry and reaction kinetics.

Through a group work with graduate students, immediate photochemical events, such as energy/electron transfer in photosynthesis, will be understood.

Students are expected to acquire ability to perform spectroscopic experiments toward such events. 

授業計画

1　Introduction: aim of this class and what we will study

2　Biopolymers and Photochemistry

3　Basic of Photochemistry 1: Absorption of light and excited states

4　Basic of Photochemistry 2: Absorption and fluorescence spectroscopies 

5　Chemistry of Photosynthesis: Excited Energy Transfer and Electron Transfer 

6　Group Work 1:  Designing Experimental theme  

7　Group Work 2: Presentation of Experimental Plans 

8　Summary and Final Exam
