授業の目的・達成目標

 

So far, solid inorganic chemistry has been mainly studied and used by researchers dealing with materials. However, in recent years it has been expected that rapid development of energy devices will develop as an energy source with low environmental impact. Solid inorganic chemistry, which forms the foundation of these technologies, is becoming a necessary field for students in more fields. In this lecture, we will explain the fundamental of advanced technology such as lithium battery and fuel cell in an easy-to-understand manner so that students other than material system can understand it, and at the same time show the possibility and problems of current new energy device. We also aim to train researchers who have knowledge of wider environmental measures by having students present their opinions on environmental technologies and environmental issues from the viewpoint of their own research fields as necessary.

The lecture is basically carried out in English, but we support it with materials etc which are not good at English.
