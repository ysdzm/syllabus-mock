授業の目的・達成目標

Employees work in their organization, concerning themselves in it. It is very significant and useful to understand human behavior in their relation with their organization because its position in an individual’s life domain is quite large. The aim of this course is to understand, explain, and forecast various behavial phenomena of human-beings and to acquire necessary knowledge, an awareness of the issues, and the ability to think for management.
In this course, students chiefly examine their own design of career paths, referring to the postwar history of Japanese economy and the history of selected enterprises, make presentations, conduct discussions, and form a final report. They will learn necessary matters from industrial organizational psychology/ organizational behavioral study, clinical social psychology, personnel affairs management/human resource management, human engineering, the postwar Japanese economy/industrial history.

授業計画

The goal of achievement is to acquire knowledge and a discerning sense concerning human management which includes organizations, groups, and human relationships. For promoting the understanding and examination of the following topics, we conduct a course by practical trainings such as students’investigation, presentations, discussions among students, and a final report.
1. human development and mentaling
2. personnel assessment and vocational aptitude
3. personnel evaluation and personnel system
4. workplace relationships
5. workplace communication
6. motivation to work and work attitude
7. decision making in an organization
8. organizational structure and form
9. organizational culture and organizational reform
10. problem solving
11. leadership and coaching
12. vocational stress and stress coping
13. human engineering and work environment
These topics will not be lectured in a direct manner.
