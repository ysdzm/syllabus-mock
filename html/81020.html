授業の目的・達成目標

[Purpose and goal of the class]

Based on the research activities organized by the individual supervisor, the purpose and goal which the students would accomplish are as follows: (a) to understand the basics of their research field and (b) to make their research plans from the aspects of both the short and long term, which would often be supported in close communication with the supervisor.

授業計画

The contents of this course include the following activities:

1. To review the publications and past literature related to their research, which the supervisors would often suggest.

2. To improve their knowledges of what they have not understood yet.

3. To plan the research goal and its schedule in both the short and long term.

4. Active participation in the presentation in the seminar and discussion is encouraged.

成績評価の方法

The course evaluation will be based on the student’s activity on their research report, discussion with the supervisor, and other members in the laboratory. In addition, attendance and presentations in the annual workshop, meeting, and conference (in some cases, international conference) are strongly encouraged and evaluated. The detailed criteria would depend on the individual supervisor of your research laboratory.

You can pass the seminar if your score is equal or larger than 60 out of 100 in total.

Punctuality and attendance are required and will be recorded. (mandatory)

The course evaluation will be based on the student’s completion of a couple of factors such as (a) to gain a deep understanding of their research, (b) to show the appropriate logical explanation on their research achievements, and (c) to clarify the assumption based on their claim and to credit the proper past literature.

事前・事後学修等の指示及び履修にあたっての注意事項

The preparation and review of your research are essential tasks to proceed with the research project, which would often be suggested by your supervisor during the course if necessary.
