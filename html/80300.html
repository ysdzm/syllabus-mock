授業の目的・達成目標

The aim of this course is to help students acquire an understanding of the fundamental principles and applications of laser technology.

 

The goals of this course are to

1. Obtain basic knowledge about characteristics of laser.

2. Understand principle of laser.

3. Understand control technology of laser beam.

4. Be able to explain the application of laser.

授業計画

 

1 Introduction - Importance of laser in industry -

2 Principle of Laser

3 Control technology of laser beam

4 Pulse laser technology

5 Measurement technology of laser pulses

6 Laser application 1 (lecture by external lecturer)

7 Laser application 2 (lecture by external lecturer)

8 Laser application 3 (lecture by external lecturer)
