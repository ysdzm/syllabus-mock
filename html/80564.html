授業の目的・達成目標

In this course, you will …

  • Gain a greater awareness of the role of sound in information transfer
  • Hone your abilities to hear the sounds around you and consider the meaning of those sounds in your life
  • Think about sound defines the relationship of an individual and the community to the environment
  • Think about how sound is used in music and speech to create particular effects and types of communication
  • Consider how sounds are created
  • Learn how to use Volume, Pan, EQ, Compression to mix sound
  • Learn how to apply reverb, delay and other effects to create space within a mix
  • Combine sounds in different projects such as a radio show and a musical composition
  • Learn about accelerated learning techniques

The grade for this course will be based on:

  • Quality of research and presentations
  • Reports

This course will be carried out primarily in English. You may choose to submit your assignments and do your presentations in either language.
