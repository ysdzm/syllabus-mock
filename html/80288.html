授業の目的・達成目標

<Subject Description>

We are living in the atmosphere and noticed that there are various kinds of flow phenomena around us. Then the study of fluid mechanics has numerous engineering applications, especially for the manufacturing. And the knowledge of the fluid phenomena is needed to solve engineering problems such as drag reduction, fluid mixing, noise reduction, and convection. Most of the complex flows appeared in the application can be presumed from the combination of fundamental arrangement of flow field. Therefore, the features of basic flow field listed in the contents are explained in the lecture. 

<Objectives>

At the beginning of this lecture, three particular topics of fundamental flows are introduced in a descriptive way in order to learn the following governing equations of motion. Then, the systematic development of the subjects of flow phenomena which are very important vortex concept in science and technology are considered. Finally, experimental methods are explained for the proper understanding of the results.

授業計画

 

(1)　Flow past a circular cylinder and Reynolds number

(2)　Free convection between parallel walls

(3)　Equation of motion and vorticity equation

(4)　Some solutions of the viscous flow equations

(5)　Boundary layers

(6)　Wakes and jets

(7)　Separation and attachment

(8) Flow in rotating fluids
